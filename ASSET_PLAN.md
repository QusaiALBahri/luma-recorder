# Luma Recorder Asset Plan

## Goal

Create assets that make Luma Recorder feel like a real startup product and help visitors understand the product in under 10 seconds.

## Required Assets

### 1. Hero Image

Purpose:

- First visual impression in README, GitHub social previews, LinkedIn posts, and launch content.

What it should show:

- Dark neon product dashboard.
- Luma Recorder name.
- Tagline: Record. Explain. Teach.
- Small visual blocks for screen, mic, webcam, and local files.
- Publisher mark: albahri.org.

Recommended format:

- `assets/hero.png`
- 1600 x 900
- dark background
- high contrast

Current placeholder:

- `assets/neon-dashboard.svg`
