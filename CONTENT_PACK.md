I launched Luma Recorder today.

It is a local-first Windows screen recorder built specifically for educators, trainers, and knowledge creators.

The problem is simple:
Many people who teach through screens do not need a full video production studio every time they want to explain something.

They need a fast way to:
- record a lesson
- explain a workflow
- add microphone narration
- include webcam when useful
- save the video locally
- polish the clip quickly

That is what Luma Recorder is built around.

Tagline:
Record. Explain. Teach.

The project is open source, portable, bilingual Arabic/English, and focused on local-first recording.

What would make a screen recorder genuinely better for teaching?

## 3. Reddit Post

```text
Title:
I built a local-first screen recorder for educators and trainers

Post:
I built Luma Recorder, a portable Windows screen recorder focused on teaching workflows.

The problem I am trying to solve:
When educators, trainers, or course creators need to explain something on screen, many tools feel heavier than the task. Sometimes the goal is just to record a short lesson, add voice or webcam, and keep the file locally.

Current features:
- full-screen recording
- selected-area recording
- microphone narration
- webcam overlay
- system audio when Windows exposes loopback
- quick trim/compress/export tools
- Arabic + English UI
- portable app folder
- no account, no upload feature, no telemetry

Repo:
https://github.com/QusaiALBahri/luma-recorder

I am looking for practical feedback from people who record lessons, training videos, or software walkthroughs.

Question:
What feature would make screen recording better for teaching?

Building a local-first screen recorder for educators
```

Outline:

1. **Intro**
   - Why teaching through screens is now a daily workflow.
   - Why many screen recorders are either too limited or too heavy.

2. **The Problem**
   - Educators need fast capture, not a production pipeline.
   - Trainers often need local files for privacy or internal workflows.
   - Course creators need microphone, webcam, and quick polish.

3. **The Product Idea**
   - Record. Explain. Teach.
   - Local-first.
   - Portable.
   - Bilingual Arabic/English.

4. **Core Features**
   - Screen capture.
   - Microphone.
   - Webcam overlay.
   - System audio where available.
   - Trim/compress/export.

5. **Privacy Decisions**
   - No account.
   - No upload.
   - No telemetry.
   - Optional Windows EFS encryption.

6. **Who It Is For**
   - Educators.
   - Trainers.
   - Knowledge creators.
   - Support teams.
   - Course creators.

7. **What Feedback Is Needed**
